How I approach technical problems

I tend to build tools around real operational friction points: cockpit workload, aviation weather awareness, compliance tracking, data visualization, and automation of repetitive workflows.

Most of my projects follow the same pattern: identify a real-world pain point, rapidly prototype a usable solution, test it in the operational environment, then refine the interface until it feels intuitive.
